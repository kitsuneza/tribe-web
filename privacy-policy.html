1. Information Collection and Use

The Application collects information when you download and use it.

In connection with the installation and usage of the app, we collect, process, and use the data described below to find out which companies, products, or other topics you are interested in and which information and offers from our partner companies are relevant for you to use the functions of our app as needed. This allows us to provide the most relevant information and offers from our partner companies in the app.

If you use the app, we collect, process, and use the following data:

  • Card data: Loyalty card and customer number to generate a barcode and protect against misuse.
  • Usage data: Information about how you use the card, such as the time, card used, and points.
  • Location data: Anonymous location-based data for app personalization and advertising.

We also use the above data in anonymized form for market research and statistical analysis.

Note: The Application does not collect precise location data from your device.

2. Data Retention

We retain data for as long as necessary to provide the Application’s services and for a reasonable period thereafter to fulfill legal or operational requirements.

If you wish to delete your data, please contact us at help@triberewardsapp.com. We will respond promptly.

3. Third-Party Services

The Application uses third-party services that have their own Privacy Policies. Below are the links to the Privacy Policies of these third-party service providers:

  • Google Play Services
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Mixpanel

We may disclose collected information in the following circumstances:

  • As required by law (e.g., to comply with a subpoena).
  • When necessary to protect rights, safety, or investigate fraud.
  • With trusted service providers who adhere to this Privacy Policy.

4. Security

We prioritize safeguarding your data and implement physical, electronic, and procedural safeguards. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is entirely secure. While we strive to protect your personal data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Opt-Out Rights

You can stop all data collection by uninstalling the Application using the standard uninstall process for your device.

6. Children

The Application is not intended for children under 13. If we discover that we have collected personal data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately. If you believe your child has provided personal information, contact us at help@triberewardsapp.com.

7. Changes

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We encourage you to review this page periodically for updates. Continued use of the Application signifies acceptance of changes.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, contact us at help@triberewardsapp.com.
